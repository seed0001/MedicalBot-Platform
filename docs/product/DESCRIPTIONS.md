## Short (tagline)

**Use for:** home page subtitle, `<meta description>`, nav hints, feature cards.

> Your health log with an assistant that never forgets a reading.

---

## Medium (landing section)

**Use for:** signup cards, onboarding intros, App Store subtitle, social bios.

> Track glucose, medications, mood, sleep, and appointments in one place. Log by chatting in plain English, spot trends over weeks, and get reminders on your Google Calendar. Choose an assistant personality that fits how you like to be spoken to — warm, direct, professional, or your own.

---

## Long (about / press)

**Use for:** About page, press kit, full App Store description, investor one-pager.

> MedicalBot is a personal health assistant that helps adults organize their own metrics, medications, and appointments. Talk to an AI that logs in plain English, adapts to a personality you choose, and keeps your full health record — conditions, prescriptions, labs, diet, exercise, and visit notes — with glossaries that explain what they mean. See patterns you would miss on paper, complete standardized assessments like PHQ-9 and GAD-7, and walk into visits with a summary already prepared. Reminders land on your real calendar, not buried in email. It is deliberately not a HIPAA product and not a medical device: honest about what it is, rigorous about what it is not. It does not diagnose, prescribe, or replace your care team — it is the organized copy you keep for yourself.
