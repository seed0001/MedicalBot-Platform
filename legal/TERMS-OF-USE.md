## 1. What MedicalBot is

MedicalBot is a **personal health management assistant** for adults who want to keep
their own health information organized. It helps you:

- **Track** health metrics over time (blood glucose, weight, blood pressure, mood, sleep,
  and more)
- **Manage** medications, schedules, and adherence
- **Remember** appointments and sync them with Google Calendar (when you choose to connect)
- **Spot patterns** in your data with charts, trends, and optional weekly summaries
- **Talk** to an AI assistant that logs what you tell it and helps you prepare for visits
- **Complete** standardized questionnaires (such as PHQ-9 or GAD-7) and see how scores
  change over time

MedicalBot is designed to be the organized notebook you wish you already had — one that
never loses a reading, never forgets a refill, and can hand your doctor a clear summary
when you ask it to.

## 3. HIPAA and your data

HIPAA protects health information handled by covered entities (hospitals, insurers, most
employer health plans) and their business associates. **MedicalBot is neither.**

That means:

- We are **not** required to meet HIPAA's full regulatory framework
- We are **not** audited or certified as HIPAA-compliant
- Your doctor's office cannot treat MedicalBot as part of their HIPAA-covered systems

**Why we tell you this plainly:** You deserve to know what you are signing up for. Many
health apps imply compliance without saying so. We would rather lose a signup than mislead
you.

**What we do anyway** (because your health data deserves respect, HIPAA or not):

- Encrypt sensitive credentials (such as Google refresh tokens) at rest
- Use HTTPS for all traffic
- Avoid logging the content of your health entries
- Let you export your data and request deletion
- Ask for Google permissions incrementally — only what each feature needs
- Treat email content from Gmail as untrusted data, never as instructions to the AI

See [PRIVACY-NOTICE.md](PRIVACY-NOTICE.md) for details on what we collect and where it
goes.

## 4. AI assistant limitations

The Service uses third-party AI models (via OpenRouter) to power conversation, data
extraction, and analysis. You acknowledge that:

- AI can make mistakes, mishear voice input, or misinterpret what you said
- AI responses are **not** medical advice
- Structured data extracted from chat should be reviewed for accuracy
- Your messages and relevant health context are sent to AI providers to generate
  responses — see the Privacy Notice for what leaves our servers
- We route different tasks to different models; you can review model settings when
  available

The assistant may alert you when a **recorded metric** crosses a threshold you configured
(for example, a blood glucose reading below 70 mg/dL). These alerts are based on data you
chose to log against a condition you told us you have. They are not a substitute for
clinical judgment.
